VidaaEdge - Custom Installation

Follow the steps below to configure your VidaaOS-based TV and install or uninstall custom apps.

Installing Apps

1: Open VidaaHub

Open the browser on your TV and navigate to: https://vidaahub.com.

2: Install/Uninstall Apps

Use the interface to install or uninstall apps. For unavailable apps in the overview, use the "+" button to add the app manually by entering the following details:

  • App Name: e.g., Jellyfin
  • App ID: Must be unique
  • App Thumbnail URL: Logo of the App.
  • App URL: URL of the app.

Tip: Use a smartphone for easier text entry.

The "Jellyfin" app is provided as an example for self-hosted installations. To use your custom Jellyfin instance, update the environment variable with your instance's URL. Hosted users, you can manually add your Jellyfin instance by using the (+) button.

3: Restart Your TV

After completing the installations or uninstallations, restart your TV. The apps will now be added or removed as per your selection.

Important Note

After completing the installations or uninstallations, you should:

  • Switch back to DHCP mode in your IP settings, OR
  • Re-enter your previous DNS server settings.
